How it works

no terminal, no keys

Files upload to secure storage, then a server-side function parses and loads them — the database key stays on the server, never in your browser. Recognized by their columns, so renamed files still land right.

Preview detects each file and shows the row counts without changing anything. Load replaces the data and re-links every clinician to their stable identity, then the console reads it live — just refresh.

Discontinued partners (Astellas, Rezilient, MedZip) are dropped automatically and flagged if they reappear. Load the full monthly set together for the cleanest refresh.

How access works

invitation-only

Provisioning pre-authorizes an email with a role. The person still signs in through the normal SteadyMD login — the moment their login exists, they're recognized here with the role you set. Until then they show as Invited.

Viewer reads every tab. Admin also manages the roster (confirm/edit/remove) and this Users list.

Revoking (Disable) blocks access immediately. You can't disable your own login or remove the last active admin.
